# retail-sales-data-analysis-dashboard
Project Overview :
This project analyzes retail transaction data to uncover insights related to revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and geographical sales performance. The objective of the analysis is to help business leaders understand key growth opportunities and support data-driven decision-making.
The analysis focuses on identifying seasonal revenue patterns, high-performing markets, and valuable customers that contribute significantly to overall revenue.

Business Questions :
The analysis was conducted to answer the following key business questions:
What are the monthly revenue trends across the year?
Which countries generate the highest revenue for the business?
Who are the top revenue-generating customers?
What potential markets offer opportunities for business expansion?

Data Cleaning :
Before performing the analysis, the dataset was cleaned to ensure accuracy and reliability.
The following steps were performed:
Removed transactions with negative quantities, as these represent product returns.
Filtered out records with missing customer IDs to ensure accurate customer analysis.
Verified data types and corrected formatting issues for numerical and date fields.
Ensured only valid sales transactions were used for visualization.

Key Insights : Revenue Trends
Monthly revenue analysis revealed seasonal sales patterns, with certain months generating significantly higher revenue.
Top Countries
The United Kingdom contributes the majority of the revenue, making it the company's primary market.
Customer Insights
A relatively small number of customers contribute a large percentage of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies.
Expansion Opportunities
Several international markets show consistent purchasing activity, indicating potential opportunities for business expansion.
